Perks

Being a sponsor also brings some advantages! The companies can enjoy the following perks during the event:

Daily Free-Parking

The company will have a paid full-day parking for one vehicle.

Lunch Discount

The company members present during the event will have the possibility to have lunch for a very low price.

Coffee-Break Access

The company members present during the event will have free access to the event’s coffee-breaks.

Wi-Fi Access

Free and unlimited access to Instituto Superior Técnico’s Wi-Fi network during the event using a company guest password.

Full Access Pass

Access to all the event’s activities: Talks, Workshops, Presentations, Tech Expo.

Statistics

Statistical data and opinions of visitors regarding the participation of the company, gathered from surveys carried out during the event.

Frequently Asked Questions

After reading this page, it is understandable that some questions were raised. It's been 25 years of SINFO and there is always something that can be explained better. Here are some of the most Frequently Asked Questions from past editions. If your question is not listed, contact us in the next section.

Is it possible to acquire an individual item?

All individual item requests should be submitted to the SINFO’s committee member assigned to your company.
Small changes to your package can also be requested for approval.

How can I download the app?

The web app is available on https://sinfo.org. You have to sign in with your Facebook, Google, LinkedIn or Fenix account.
The app will allow you to scan the students' QR codes, download the Curricula and write notes about the attendees.
In order to avoid past issues, please ask permission to your company to sign in with one of those options.

Is the Diamond Package exclusive for one company?

Yes, the Diamond Package is for our Main Sponsor, therefore it is only possible to have one company. If there is more than one company interested in this package, the package will be auctioned by closed letter to deliver until a deadline to be defined.

Can you explain the speed dates better?

How it works
Many companies requested some quiet space where they can do a quick interview with the students who seemed valuable. SINFO 26 will have a special space dedicated to this "first impression interviews". Companies will be able to schedule with the students a 15 minutes slot to interview them.

The deal
The speed dates are an experiment taking place this edition. It will only be available for Platinum and Diamond Packages, with no additional cost. If it succeeds, it will be definitively included in our product, this time with additional cost.

How should I design the presentation/workshop’s name?

The names should not exceed 30 characters, and, at the same time, should be appealing and descriptive of the tematics discussed during the session. According to our statistics the companies who follow these guidelines end up having a highly crowded session.

In which format should I send the logo of the company?

The logo should be sent in vectorial format (.ai, .svg, .eps, .pdf, etc.). If none of those is available, you should send us a .png with the maximum resolution available.
If the company has different logos *for different color backgrounds, you should send us all of the possible options.
